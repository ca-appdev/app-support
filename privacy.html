Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tap Rush mobile app ("Tap Rush", "we", "us") handles information. By using Tap Rush you agree to this policy.

The short version

Tap Rush has no accounts and no sign-up. Your game progress is stored only on your device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal information ourselves. The app does use a small number of third-party services (Google AdMob for ads, and Apple's Game Center and in-app purchases), described below.

Information we collect directly

None. Tap Rush does not ask for your name, email address, contacts, photos, or precise location, and it has no login. Your scores, coins, unlocked skins, streaks, and settings are saved locally on your device using standard iOS storage and are removed when you delete the app.

Third-party services

Google AdMob (advertising). Tap Rush shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google may collect and process information such as device identifiers (including the Advertising Identifier / IDFA where you allow it), advertising and usage data, approximate (coarse) location, and diagnostics. This data is handled under Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy. On iOS, the app asks for tracking permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt; you can decline, and you can change this any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. You can also limit personalized ads in your device settings. Buying Remove Ads or the Starter Pack removes banner and interstitial ads.

Apple Game Center (optional leaderboards). If you are signed in to Game Center, your high scores and Sprint times may be submitted to Apple to rank you on leaderboards. This is handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. If you are not signed in, no scores are submitted.

Apple in-app purchases. Purchases (such as coin packs or Remove Ads) are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles your payment; we do not receive or store your payment details. The app only learns whether a purchase succeeded so it can unlock the item.

Children

Tap Rush is rated for general audiences (4+) and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will address it.

Data retention and deletion

Because game data lives on your device, you can delete all of it at any time by deleting the app. Any data processed by the third parties above is retained according to their own policies, linked above.

Your choices

  • Decline ad tracking at the App Tracking Transparency prompt, or turn it off in iOS Settings.
  • Limit personalized advertising in your device privacy settings.
  • Remove ads via an in-app purchase.
  • Delete the app to erase all locally stored game data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and post the new version on this page.
