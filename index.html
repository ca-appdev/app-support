Tap Rush

Tap the dark tiles. Don't miss.

Tap Rush is a fast, one-thumb reflex game for iPhone. Tap the dark tile in every row, never miss, and chase your best score as the tiles speed up. Play Endless or race the clock in Sprint, arm power-ups, grab gold tiles, and climb the leaderboards.
