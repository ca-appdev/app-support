Frequently asked questions

How do I remove ads?
Open the Shop (cart icon on the menu) and choose Remove Ads, or the Starter Pack, which removes ads and includes bonus coins and a skin.

I bought something on a new phone — how do I get it back?
Open the Shop and tap Restore Purchases. This restores non-consumable purchases such as Remove Ads and the Starter Pack. Consumable coin packs are added to your balance when bought and can't be restored.

Where is my progress stored?
Your scores, coins, skins and settings are saved locally on your device. Deleting the app erases this data. There is no account or cloud sync.

The leaderboards or "Ranks" button aren't showing.
Tap Rush uses Apple Game Center. Make sure you're signed in to Game Center in the iOS Settings app, then reopen Tap Rush.

How does Sprint mode work?
Sprint is a race to clear a set number of tiles as fast as you can, across four levels (30, 60, 100 and 150 tiles). Finish a level to unlock the next one; each keeps your best time.

How do power-ups work?
On the menu you can arm power-ups before a run using coins: Shield (extra lives), 2× Score, and Slow Start. They apply to your next run.
